Problem 8

Category: N/A

Agnes writes the following four statements on a blank piece of paper. $\bullet$ At least one of these statements is true. $\bullet$ At least two of these statements are true. $\bullet$ At least two of these statements are false. $\bullet$ At least one of these statements is false. Each statement is either true or false. How many false statements did Agnes write on the paper? $$\textbf{(A) } 0 \qquad\textbf{(B) } 1 \qquad\textbf{(C) } 2 \qquad\textbf{(D) } 3 \qquad\textbf{(E) } 4$$

Difficulty: 13/100 — A clean logic puzzle; a step down from the previous misplaced early problems.

Core Concepts: Systemically work through the statements.

Non-obvious Transitions: N/A

Techniques: Test each scenario by number of true statements.

Error-prone Steps: Getting lost in circular reasoning.

Ideal Time: Experienced contestants may take up to $1$ minute to complete the problem. Intermediate contestants may take up to $1$ minute to complete the problem. Beginner contestants may take up to $1.5-2$ minutes to solve the problem.
