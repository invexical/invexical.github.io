invexic's Competition Difficulty Guide

Difficulty Scale (0–100)

0–10 Very Easy Immediate, 1-step, minimal reasoning.
11–20 Easy Straightforward; early AMC problems. They are often conceptual.
21–35 Easy–Medium Some structure required; mild trick or observation required.
36–50 Medium Multi-step problem-solving, but still approachable.
51–65 Medium–Hard Non-standard approaches; more involved. Requires more advanced concepts.
66–80 Hard Later AMC problems; tricky even for advanced contestants.
81–90 Very Hard Top-end AMC difficulty; requires ingenuity or multiple observations.
91–100 Extremely Hard AIME-level problem-solving or difficult bashes.

About this Guide

I've detailed five main points for each problem in this difficulty guide: core ideas, non-obvious transitions, techniques, error-prone steps, and time costs.
